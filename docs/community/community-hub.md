# Community Hub

Join the QuartumSE community to collaborate on experiments, share insights, and get support.

## Connect

- **Developer discussions:** Participate in issue triage and roadmap planning on the [QuartumSE GitHub repository](https://github.com/QuartumSE/quartumse).
- **Live support sessions:** Coordinate pair-programming or debugging sessions via the community calendar (coming soon).
- **Announcements:** Subscribe to the updates feed announced in the [Educational Videos](../home/educational-videos.md) page.

## Support Resources

- Review the [Quickstart tutorial](../tutorials/quickstart.md) before opening a support ticket to ensure prerequisites are covered.
- Consult the [CLI reference](../reference/cli.md) and [API docs](../reference/api/index.md) for usage details.
- When reporting an issue, link relevant manifests or reports generated via the [replay](../how-to/replay-from-manifest.md) and [report generation](../how-to/generate-report.md) guides.

## Collaborate on Research

- Coordinate experiment ownership through the [experiment tracker](../research/experiment-tracker.md).
- Share literature additions and critiques via the [literature library](../research/literature.md).
- Surface cross-team initiatives—like benchmarking or outreach—on the [partnerships board](partnerships.md).

## Contribute Documentation

- Propose new tutorials or corrections by following the contribution guidelines in `CONTRIBUTING.md`.
- Tag documentation pull requests with the `docs` label so they appear in the next community sync.
